About Me

Let me introduce myself. I'm Hendy Juannathan Revel Wibowo, an active Civil Engineering student with a passion and dedication to developing in the world of construction and mapping. ...
My experience as a professional surveyor has honed my technical skills and broadened my knowledge of land measurement, topographic mapping, and the documentation of various infrastructure projects. Armed with perseverance and a motivation to continue learning, I have been trusted to lead and be involved in important projects such as toll road construction, the widening of the iconic Malioboro area, and the land certification process.
I also actively contribute to campus organizations and activities, from teaching assistants and event moderators to organizing various engineering and sports competitions. Each experience has provided valuable opportunities to work in teams, communicate effectively, and face challenges with creativity and innovative solutions.
I believe that hard work, enthusiasm, and collaboration are key to making a positive impact in the field of civil engineering. I am open to collaborative opportunities and ready to contribute to various challenging projects to build a better future through tangible work in the construction world.
